# Password Reset Knowledge Base

## Overview
This article explains how users can reset their enterprise account password and regain access to systems connected through Single Sign-On.

## Common Symptoms
Users may report that they forgot their password, their account is locked, or they cannot log in after changing their password.

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Ask the user to visit the enterprise Single Sign-On password reset portal.
2. Verify the user's identity using the approved recovery method.
3. Reset the password and wait 5 to 10 minutes for synchronization.
4. Ask the user to sign out of all active sessions.
5. Ask the user to log in again using the new password.
6. If the account is locked, wait for the lockout period or escalate to Identity and Access Management.

## Escalation
If the user cannot reset the password or the account remains locked, route the ticket to Identity and Access Management.

## Ticket Category
Account Access

## Assigned Team
Identity and Access Management
